Frequently asked

Do I need an account?

No. Solo habit tracking works fully offline with no account. You only need an account to use the Arena — the private competition feature.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to your profile/settings, and choose delete account. This removes your cloud profile and associated Arena data. Uninstalling the app removes the data stored on your device.

Is my data private?

Yes. Solo data stays on your device. Account data is protected so only you (and the challenges you're part of) can access it. We don't sell data or run ad trackers. See our Privacy Policy.
